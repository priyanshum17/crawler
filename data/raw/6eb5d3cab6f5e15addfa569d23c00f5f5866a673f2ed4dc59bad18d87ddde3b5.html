Celebrating Women in Computing

Creating the Next Generation

It’s a common refrain among forward-thinking computer scientists: If our researchers and programmers innovating solutions for the world don’t look like the rest of the world, then they aren’t really creating solutions for the world. That’s why the College of Computing makes central to its mission the recruitment, education, and promotion of women in the field. In this celebration of women in computing, we hope to highlight the significant contributions of our faculty, staff, students, and alumni, while keeping an eye toward the work left to be done.
