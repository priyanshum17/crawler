Vivek Sarkar is John P. Imlay, Jr. Dean of the College of Computing at Georgia Tech, and a professor in the School of Computer Science.  He conducts research in multiple aspects of parallel computing including programming languages, compilers, runtime systems, and debugging and verification systems for high performance computers.  For more details on Prof. Sarkar’s background, please click on the Bio & CV link on the right.  

There are openings available for students interested in research in Prof. Sarkar’s lab at Georgia Tech.  Interested applicants should send email to Prof. Sarkar, and cc: his assistant, Cynthia Bryant. Georgia Tech students should also feel free to message Prof. Sarkar via Teams.
